About Us

We are a group of four students from Grinnell College, Iowa, United States. The idea for luatsu.tech was inspired by Dung Bui's difficulty with seeking legal help in Vietnam, from which he noticed there was no existing infrastructure that would make the process efficient. The traditional way of finding a lawyer through existing contacts limits the seeker's options and lawyers' potential customer base. Using social media is time-consuming, insecure and dependent on individual lawyer's availability.


This signifies the question we are trying to answer - how to help uninformed individuals get help fastest, cheapest, most secure and how to help quality lawyers reach out to a larger audience. Taking advantage of the Internet's rise in Vietnam, we decided to use our existing resources to put the idea into real life and turn the legal industry into an efficient marketplace.
