Immigration



TimLuatSu provides immigration help at a fixed price. We guide you through the process of finding immigration help—from getting a green card to applying for US citizenship. No matter where you are in the process, Avvo Legal Services can help.

If you want to become a permanent US resident, you need a green card. You can apply for a green card through a family member, through a job, or if you are seeking refuge or asylum in the United States. The most common way is through a family member or if you’re being sponsored by a company or organization for employment in the US. There are a few other special situations that can qualify you for a green card. If you have questions about your immigration situation, working with an immigration lawyer can help you get on the right track.

If you want more rights and protection than a permanent resident with a US green card, you need to apply for US citizenship. Being a US citizen means you’re exempt from being deported, you can vote, sponsor relatives who want to immigrate, and more. This gives you almost all of the rights as a natural-born US citizen. To become a US citizen, you need to meet specific requirements. One of those requirements is that you typically need to be a permanent resident for several years. The number of years depends on different factors, like if you’ve been married to a US citizen and for how long.
